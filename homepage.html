Crime is terribly revealing. Try and vary your methods as you will, your tastes, your habits, your attitude of mind, and your soul is revealed by your actions.

Ten strangers are lured to an island mansion off the coast. They are murdered one by one. The killer is among them and is ready to strike...


In attempt to create an interactive narrative based on the last chapter of And Then There Were None by English writer Agatha Christie, this web experience visualizes the crime scene as well as the postscript from the murderer.

You are invited

DR. ARMSTRONG is paid to check the health condition of Mrs. and Mr. Owen.


ANTHONY MARSTON is lured to the island by the prospect of having a good time and looking for a party and pretty girls.


EMILY BRENT is invited to the island for a free vacation.


MRS. ROGERS is hired through an agency as a cook and maid.


GENERAL MACARTHUR is invited to visit with old army buddies.

JUSTICE LAWRENCE received a letter from an old friend.


PHILIP LOMBARD is hired to be at the disposal of Mr. Owen.


MR. ROGERS is hired through an agency as a butler.


VERA CLAYTHORNE is hired to be the secretary for this summer to Mrs. Owen.


WILLIAM BLORE is hired to keep an eye on the guests and the jewels of Mrs. Owen.
