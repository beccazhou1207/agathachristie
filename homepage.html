Crime is terribly revealing. Try and vary your methods as you will, your tastes, your habits, your attitude of mind, and your soul is revealed by your actions.
Agatha Christie

Ten strangers are lured to an island mansion off the coast. They are murdered one by one. The killer is among them and is ready to strike...


In attempt to create an interactive narrative based on the last chapter of And Then There Were None by English writer Agatha Christie, this web experience visualizes the crime scene as well as the postscript from the murderer.

↳7 Little Soldier boys

↳4 Little Soldier boys

↳3 Little Soldier boys
Three little Soldier boys walking in the zoo; A big bear hugged one and then there were two.

Two little Soldier boys playing with a gun; One shot the other and then there was One.
↳1 Little Soldier boy
One little Soldier boy left all alone; He went out and hanged himself and then there were none.
