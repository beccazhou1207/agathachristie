And Then There
Were None

Ten strangers are lured to an island mansion off the coast. They are murdered one by one. The killer is among them and he is ready to strike again and again...

This website creates an interactive narrative based on the last chapter of And Then There Were None by English writer Agatha Christie.

The web experience visualizes the crime scene as well as the postscript from the killer.
