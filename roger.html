R o g e r s, T h o m a s

I killed Rogers on the morning of August 10th.

He was chopping sticks for lighting the fire and did not hear me approach. I found the key to the dining-room door in his pocket. He had locked it the night before.

In the confusion attending the finding of Rogers' body. I slipped into Lombard's room and abstracted his revolver.
I knew that he would have one with him-in fact, I had instructed Morris to suggest when he interviewed him.
